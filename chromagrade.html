Chromagrade

In the summer of 2023, I traveled to the Venture Labs of the University of Pennsylvania to embark on an entrepeneurial journey. With a love for films and photography I wanted to address the issue of budgeting and inaccessibilty when it comes to filmmaking. Through a rigorous 3-step ideation tournament, my idea, Chromagrade reached the final 10 ideas, achieving first place in the semifinals and second in the final symposium, valuating at $6.7 million.
