OpticTune

OpticTune is a startup I founded after struggling immensely with my lazy eye (poor ocular muscles). Using a simple mechanical creation, I trained my eyes to exterminate my disease. Motivated by its success, I have been partnering with local ocular centers to provide patients with the same engineering-based therapy.
